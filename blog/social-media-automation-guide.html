Platform-specific safety and warm-up patterns for Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, and Twitter/X.

Quick Overview

Use one profile per account, match proxy geolocation with language/timezone, and apply a conservative warm-up sequence before performing automation. Cloud Phones and human-like delays reduce account risk.

Warm-up Sequences

  1. Open the profile and visit the platform homepage for 3–7 minutes.
  2. Perform low-risk actions: like, view, small scrolls, and profile updates over 2–5 sessions.
  3. Gradually increase activity and introduce automation after 24–72 hours of warm-up.

Platform-specific Tips

Instagram

Prefer mobile UA and Cloud Phones for uploads; use realistic posting cadence and limit follows per hour.

Facebook

Use consistent friends list signals; avoid sudden ad campaign spikes from brand-new accounts.

TikTok

Use human-like video interactions and staggered uploads; avoid bulk follows and mass messaging.

Automation & AI

Combine Multilogin with Playwright/Puppeteer and humanized timing. Use randomized delays, mouse movements, and realistic scrolling patterns.

Team Workflows

Use shared cloud folders for team profiles, enforce strict tagging and proxy ownership per profile, and audit session logs regularly.
