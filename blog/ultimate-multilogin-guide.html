Fingerprints & Consistency

Trust Multilogin's autogenerated fingerprints for most uses. If you manually edit properties (Canvas, WebGL, Fonts), ensure consistency across language, timezone, and proxy location. Inconsistencies are a major signal to detection systems.

Proxy Strategy

Use one dedicated proxy per profile. Avoid sharing proxies across unrelated accounts. When possible, match the proxy geolocation to the profile's language and timezone settings.
